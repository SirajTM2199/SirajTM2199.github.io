Aspiring Computer Vision & Medical Imaging Researcher

I am a BSMS student at IISER Thiruvananthapuram, majoring in Data Science. I am currently pursuing my master's thesis on Quantitative Susceptibility Mapping (QSM) reconstruction, under the supervision of Dr. Raji Susan Mathew.

My research interests include deep learning for inverse problems in medicine, deep reinforcement learning and computer vision. My master's thesis focused on deep reinforcement learning for QSM reconstruction, where we developed an iterative voxel-wise deep reinforcement learning paradigm for QSM reconstruction. I have also worked on change captioning for bitemporal remote sensing imagery using State-Space models guided by Graph Neural Networks (GNNs).

Outside of research, I enjoy debating, hiking, and reading. More about my work can be found in the publications section. For collaborations or inquiries, feel free to get in touch.
